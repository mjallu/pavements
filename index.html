About Pavement Management Systems

Pavement Management Systems is a premier ISO 9001:2015 certified multi-disciplinary engineering consulting company specializing in pavement engineering and infrastructure development. Established with a vision to provide world-class pavement solutions, we have become a trusted name in the industry.

Our expertise spans across highways, airports, urban roads, and industrial pavements. We utilize state-of-the-art equipment including Network Survey Vehicles (NSV), Falling Weight Deflectometer (FWD), Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR), and advanced laboratory testing facilities.

With our NABL accredited laboratory and experienced team of engineers, we deliver comprehensive solutions from conceptual design to project completion, ensuring quality, safety, and sustainability in every project.
